Privacy

How student information is handled in this eligibility checker.

Information collected

The app stores account details and profile information entered by the student, such as state, category, education level, income range, marks, certificate readiness, and scholarship-related conditions.

Purpose

This information is used to calculate likely scholarship eligibility and show missing requirements. It should not be sold or used for unrelated marketing.

Sensitive documents

The current version records whether a student has documents. It does not upload or store certificate files, Aadhaar numbers, bank account numbers, or original documents.

Admin access

Site administrators can manage scheme data and may access student profiles through Django Admin. Production deployments should restrict admin accounts to trusted operators only.
