Terms

Important limits of the eligibility checker.

Eligibility guidance

Results are generated from scheme rules stored in the database. They are guidance only and do not guarantee approval, selection, or payment.

Official verification

Students must verify all rules, deadlines, documents, and application steps on the official government portal before applying.

Data accuracy

Eligibility depends on accurate profile information and up-to-date scheme data. Admins should review scheme rules regularly using official sources.

Deployment responsibility

Anyone deploying this project should configure a strong secret key, secure admin credentials, HTTPS, backups, and appropriate privacy controls.
